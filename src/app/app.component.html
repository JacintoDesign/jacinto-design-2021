Zero To Mastery

Hi there! I'm Jacinto, a Senior Developer at the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation with a diverse background that includes 5 years of experience as a teacher in both Canada and South Korea. My journey into the world of technology started at a young age when I built my first computer at 12 and delved into design software at 16.

Currently, I wear the hat of an instructor at the Zero to Mastery Academy. In this role, I leverage my artistic and critical thinking skills to craft high-quality projects that significantly enhance students' portfolios, giving them a head start in their web development careers.
